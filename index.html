Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation solutions

Advanced Physiotherapy

Our advanced physiotherapy services utilize cutting-edge techniques and evidence-based practices to treat a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, sports injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

  • Manual therapy and mobilization
  • Exercise prescription and rehabilitation
  • Pain management techniques
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Sports injury treatment
  • Ergonomic assessments

Neurophysiotherapy

Specialized neurophysiotherapy services designed to help patients with neurological conditions regain function, improve mobility, and enhance their quality of life through targeted interventions.

  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Spinal cord injury management
  • Parkinson's disease therapy
  • Multiple sclerosis treatment
  • Balance and gait training
  • Functional electrical stimulation
